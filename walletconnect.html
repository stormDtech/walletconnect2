WalletConnect

Walletconnect is an Open protocol made to facilitate a secure connection between mobile cryptocurrency wallets and desktop application, such as Dapps. Transactions are made through an encrypted source protocol for connecting decentralized applications to mobile wallets with QR code scanning or deep linking. as a private key never leaves the user's device, their funds are never at risk and the chances of a hijack is very low. Users can interact securely with any Dapp from their mobile phone, making WalletConnect wallets a safer choice compared to desktop or browser extension wallets.

How does it work?

Interact with all of web3. We facilitate a secure connection between wallets and hundreds of apps, bridging you to DeFi, NFTs, and so much more.

Sign off on your activity. You'll be promoted to verify each transactionin a seamless, one-click experience.

Step into the multi-chain future. You can connect to a wallet with one or more chains simultaneously and send transactions to different chains at the same time.
No more chain switching!

Just one log-in for all of web3. You can now say goodbye to endless accounts! Walletconnect provides the infrastructure for you to connect to all of web3 with your prefered wallet.

Connects with minimal clicks. All you need to do is scan a QR code or click on a deep link to connect with an app. WalletConnect gets to work, establishing the remote pairing in the background.

Get pinged when updates occur. Notification are broadcasted directly to your wallet ensuring that you never miss out on an opportunity again.

Authenticate your wallet/Apps. Authenticate your wallet/App with WalletConnect to qualify for Airdrops/ Minting
